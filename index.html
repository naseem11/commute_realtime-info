About

This application provides the realtime information for Dublin Bus , Irish Rail and Dublin Bike .

Getting Bus Information

It gives the list of buses with their due times for any bus stop searched by the user. There are three ways to get the real time information , by stop number like 312, by address like Wellington Quey or by route number like 25A ,7 etc.


Getting Rail Information

You can also get the realtime information for Irish rail service.You just need to enter the station name to get the status of all trains serving that station


Getting Dublin Bike Information

Status of bikes for any station can be checked by the station number or address.
